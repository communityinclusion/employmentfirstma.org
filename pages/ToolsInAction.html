Massachusetts Tools in Action is

  • A virtual community to share information with others on Charting the LifeCourse
  • An opportunity to learn how colleagues across Massachusetts are using Charting the LifeCourse Framework and Tools, to brainstorm and get ideas from others, and to share promising practices.
  • A learning community open to anyone who is interested.

Tools in Action is a collaboration between the Arc of Massachusetts, Massachusetts Department of Developmental Services, and the Institute for Community Inclusion.

Monthly Tools in Action webinars take place from 12pm-1pm on the fourth Wednesday of every month.

Tools in Action is open to anyone who wants to join; to access the Zoom link, please register here .

The Charting the LifeCourse framework is based on the core belief that all people and their families have the right to live, love, learn, work, play and pursue their life aspirations in their community. The Charting the Life Course framework and tools can be used by individuals, families, and professionals to envision and chart a course toward a good life. Charting the Life Course is being adopted throughout Massachusetts in various ways.

To learn more about Charting the Life Course activities in Massachusetts visit: thearcofmass.org/lifecourse and supportstofamilies.org/teams/massachusetts/.

To learn more about Charting the Life Course more generally, see the Life Course Nexus, hosted by the University of Missouri Kansas City: https://www.lifecoursetools.com/
