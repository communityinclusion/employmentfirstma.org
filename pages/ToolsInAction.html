The Charting the LifeCourse framework is based on the core belief that all people and their families have the right to live, love, learn, work, play and pursue their life aspirations in their community.

Massachusetts LifeCourse in Action is:

  • a virtual community to share information with others on the Charting the LifeCourse Framework and Tools
  • an opportunity to learn how people across Massachusetts are using the Charting the LifeCourse Framework and Tools in their life and work, to brainstorm and get ideas from others, and to share promising practices
  • a learning community open to anyone who is interested, including individuals, families, and professionals
  • a collaboration between the Arc of Massachusetts, Massachusetts Department of Developmental Services, and the Institute for Community Inclusion

Individuals, families, and professionals can use the Charting the LifeCourse framework and tools to envision and chart a course toward a good life. Throughout Massachusetts, Charting the LifeCourse is being adopted in various ways.
